Adoption FAQ

Common questions about our adoption process

What are the adoption fees?

Fees range from $150-$300 depending on the animal, covering vaccinations, microchipping, and spay/neuter surgery.

How long does the process take?

Typically 3-7 days from application to adoption, depending on home check scheduling.

Can I adopt if I rent?

Yes, we require proof of landlord approval for pet ownership in rental properties.

What if it doesn't work out?

We offer a 30-day trial period and will take the animal back at any time if needed.
