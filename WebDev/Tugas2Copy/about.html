About FluffTowns

Our journey of compassion, sustainability, and creating better lives for animals in need.

Our Story

Founded in 2017 with a vision for compassionate animal care

FluffTowns Sanctuary was established by Win Metawin, a veterinarian who recognized the need for a shelter that combined exceptional animal care with environmental responsibility.

What started as a small rescue operation has grown into a leading animal welfare organization, rescuing over 2,500 animals and maintaining an 88% adoption success rate.

Our eco-friendly facility features solar panels, rainwater harvesting, and sustainable materials, reflecting our commitment to both animal welfare and environmental stewardship.

Every day, we work tirelessly to provide medical care, rehabilitation, and love to animals who have been abandoned, neglected, or abused.
